A Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi (2 October 1869 - 30 January 1948)

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was a freedom fighter and a political leader who played a dominant role in India's struggle for Independence. Mahatma Gandhi was instrumental in liberating India from British rule. Gandhi's early life was ordinary but he became a great man during the course of his life. Mahatma Gandhi is followed by millions, for he proved that one can become a great soul during the course of one's life, should they possess the will to do so.
